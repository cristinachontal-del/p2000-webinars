Built for Unmanned Systems Operators

From prototype airframes to deployed fleets, Pentagon 2000 supports the teams designing, building, maintaining and sustaining unmanned aircraft.

UAV & Drone Manufacturing

Plan and build airframes, payloads and ground control systems with full BOM, MRP, work order and kitting control, so production stays on schedule and on spec.

Payload & Avionics Integration

Manage fast-moving sensors, avionics and electronic components with the traceability and procurement control that complex integration work demands.

Defense, ISR & Surveillance

Run program-driven operations for defense and surveillance fleets, with the configuration control, documentation and audit trail these contracts demand.

MRO & Fleet Sustainment

Track component life limits, inspections, repairs and overhauls across an entire unmanned fleet from a single maintenance record.

Flexible Deployment

On-Premise

Deploy Pentagon 2000 within your own IT infrastructure for organizations that require direct control over security, data management, compliance and system administration.

Cloud

Pentagon Upstream delivers Pentagon 2000 through a managed cloud environment hosted on AWS, reducing infrastructure overhead while maintaining enterprise-level capabilities.

Browser-Based
